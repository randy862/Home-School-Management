# Discovery Notes

## Problem Statement
Home-school operators need a simple way to manage learners, lessons, attendance, and progress without heavy SIS complexity.

## Initial MVP Hypothesis
A focused dashboard with student roster, lesson planner, and attendance/progress capture will cover the first-use value.

## Open Questions
- Should MVP be single-family first or multi-family capable?
- Is offline-first support required in phase 1?
- Which reports matter most in first release?
