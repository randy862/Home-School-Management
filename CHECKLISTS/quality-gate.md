# Quality Gate Checklist

## Concepts
- [ ] Requirements are correct and complete.
- [ ] Solution approach is coherent and feasible.
- [ ] Edge cases and constraints are addressed.

## Elements
- [ ] Required files and sections are present.
- [ ] Formatting and interface rules are followed.
- [ ] Ownership and dependencies are explicit.

## Content
- [ ] Writing is clear and actionable.
- [ ] Examples or validation steps are included.
- [ ] Risks and assumptions are documented.

## Perfection Loop
- [ ] Rubric applied.
- [ ] Scored on 10-point scale.
- [ ] Revised until 10/10.
