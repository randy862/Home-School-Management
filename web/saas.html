FAQ

Questions customers will likely ask before subscribing.

What happens right after payment?

Your subscription is confirmed, the platform starts tenant provisioning, and you are shown a provisioning status path instead of being left to guess what happens next.

How will I receive access?

The system will deliver your hosted URL and onboarding instructions when the tenant is ready, including setup-token or admin access details depending on the final launch flow.

Will billing be handled securely?

Yes. The first recommended implementation uses Stripe Checkout so payment details are handled through Stripe rather than a custom card-entry flow.

What if there is a billing issue later?

The subscription lifecycle includes past-due, grace-period, suspension, and resume states so billing enforcement can be managed clearly without confusing runtime health and commercial status.
